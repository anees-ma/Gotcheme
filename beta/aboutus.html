Who we are

Gotche Middle East F.Z.E is a representative office of Barrelyene International, a US based company involved in Performance Chemicals and Additives used for Home & Personal Care, Water Treatment, Oil & Gas and Construction Industries.
The company holds commercial license in Ajman Free Zone for importing, handling, storing and selling of chemicals and related services.

Our Mission

To provide our client with competitive prices, quality materials and good services. Provide a rewarding work environment for our employees, utilizing international best practices and to live by our fundamental values.

Our Vision

To be the preferred supplier to our customers for sustainable systems and solutions and work as a catalyst to improve the core industries.
